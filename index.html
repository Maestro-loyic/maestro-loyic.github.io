Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Hope

Elimu Foundation Africa Limited is a Ugandan non-profit organisation headquartered in Kawempe, Kampala. For over a decade, we have walked alongside communities — helping children stay in school, giving women the tools they need to thrive, and strengthening the spiritual fabric of our society.

Our name, Elimu, means "Education" in Swahili — and education in its broadest sense is at the heart of everything we do.
