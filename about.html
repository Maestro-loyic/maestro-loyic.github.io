Our Story

From Komamboga
to East Africa

Elimu Foundation Africa Limited was founded in Kawempe Municipality, Kampala, with a simple but powerful vision: to initiate and sustain programs that make a real difference in the lives of the most vulnerable.

Starting in the streets of Komamboga Parish, we began by identifying children at risk of dropping out of school and connecting them with resources, mentors, and community support. Over the years the work expanded to women's empowerment, church partnerships, and youth leadership.

Today, Elimu Foundation Africa operates across multiple communities in Uganda with a growing footprint in East Africa — always returning to its roots of genuine, grassroots, community-led change.
