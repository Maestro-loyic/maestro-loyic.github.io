Privacy Policy

How Elimu Foundation Africa Limited collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026

Elimu Foundation Africa Limited ("Elimu Foundation," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices available to you. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us, such as when you:

  • Fill in the contact form on our Contact page (name, email, phone, and message).
  • Sign up for our newsletter (email address, first and last name).
  • Correspond with us directly by phone, email, or in person.

We do not currently process online donations or payments directly through this website. If that changes, this policy will be updated to describe how payment information is handled and by which processor.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To send newsletter updates to subscribers who have opted in.
  • To improve our programs, communications, and this website.
  • To maintain records required for our operations as a non-profit organisation in Uganda.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Analytics

This website may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site (for example, which pages are most viewed) so we can improve it. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors personally.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

  • You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.
  • You may request that we correct or delete personal information we hold about you by emailing joemarg2013@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

While our programs serve children in our communities, this website does not knowingly collect personal information online directly from children. Any stories, images, or details about children shared on this site are provided with appropriate consent from parents, guardians, or caregivers.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites (for example, social media platforms or Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us:

  • Email: joemarg2013@gmail.com
  • Phone: +256 772 421 468
  • Address: Waliggo Road, Komamboga Central Zone, Kawempe, Kampala, Uganda
