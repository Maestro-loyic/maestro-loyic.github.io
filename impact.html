Our Journey

Milestones Along the Way

From one neighbourhood in Komamboga to communities across Uganda.

2013

Foundation Established

Registered and begins working with 12 families in Komamboga Parish, identifying children at risk of dropping out of school and connecting them with support.

2015

Women's Program Launch

15 women begin tailoring training. Within one year, 10 have started home-based businesses transforming their family incomes.

2017

Church Partnership Network

Formal partnerships with five local churches established. Community outreach reaches over 800 residents in a single year.

2019

School Infrastructure Support

First classroom rehabilitation completed at a primary school in Kawempe, benefiting over 300 students. Three more schools added to the support network.

2021

Post-Pandemic Recovery

COVID-19 school closures threatened years of progress. Emergency food support, digital learning kits, and psychosocial support delivered across Kawempe.

2023

Youth Leadership Program

First cohort of 40 young people aged 15–25 enrolled. Graduates now lead community initiatives independently.

2025

Expanding Regional Reach

Programs active in 30+ communities across Kampala and Central Uganda, with plans extending into Eastern Uganda and East Africa.
