Programs

Programs That
Change Lives

Four focused areas of work, each designed to address a critical gap and deliver lasting transformation in the communities we serve.

Program 01

School Support Program

Education is the foundation of every other form of progress. Our School Support Program works directly with primary and secondary schools in Kawempe and surrounding areas to ensure children stay in school, learn effectively, and graduate.

We provide school fees for vulnerable children, supply learning materials, train teachers, build or rehabilitate school infrastructure, and establish mentorship circles that guide students through critical academic milestones.

Beyond classrooms, we run after-school clubs that develop life skills — communication, problem-solving, and leadership — so students are prepared not just for exams but for life.

Education Children Schools
Program 02

Women Skills Empowerment

When a woman is empowered, an entire family is transformed. Our Women Empowerment Program provides practical vocational training in tailoring and fashion design, soap and candle making, food processing and catering, and small business entrepreneurship.

Beyond skills, we offer microfinance linkages, savings group formation, and ongoing mentorship so women can turn new skills into sustainable livelihoods.

We also run a Women's Health and Wellbeing component addressing maternal health, nutrition, and mental health awareness.

Empowerment Livelihoods Women
Program 03

Church Community Outreach

Faith communities are some of the most powerful forces for social cohesion in Uganda. Our Church Program partners with local congregations to run community outreach days, inter-church fellowships, youth discipleship, and community reconciliation dialogues.

We also support church-based counselling services for families going through hardship, and provide training for pastors and church leaders to be more effective in their community support roles.

This program recognises that lasting change requires not just skills and resources, but also spiritual grounding and community belonging.

Faith Community Outreach
Program 04

Youth Leadership Development

The next generation of community leaders is already among us. Our Youth Leadership Program identifies young people aged 15–25 with potential and invests in their development through leadership camps, civic engagement training, and peer education initiatives.

Young leaders learn public speaking, project management, community mobilisation, and conflict resolution. Many go on to become volunteers, program ambassadors, and change-makers in their own communities — multiplying the impact of everything we do.
